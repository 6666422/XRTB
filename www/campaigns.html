Campaign Management Tutorials

Overview - In this section an overview of the Campaign Administration process is discussed. The topics covered are the relationship of Templates to Banner ads,using multiple creatives in a single campaign, and attribute parsing to make sure your campaigns are selecting the right bid requests. Also covered is how to format your image, pixel tracking and forward urls in the creatives

Edit Existing Campaign with Banner Creatives - An existing banner ad based campaign is edited showing how to construct the creatives, set up constraints, and set the price and other attributes of the campaign

Create a new Banner Campaigns - A new banner ad based campaign is created from scratch

Editing an Existing Campaign with Video Creatives - An existing campaign that includes a video campaign is edited, showing how to add constraints to the creative, and what the video tag looks like.

Creating a New Campaign - A new campaign is created and both a video and banner-ad creatives are constructed for this campaign
